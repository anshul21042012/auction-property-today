Frequently Answered Queries

Essential underwriting standards and claim policies to qualify for prime health, life, and asset coverages.

Under Indian IT Acts, premiums paid for Health Insurance qualify for deduction up to **₹25,000 (₹50,000 for senior citizens)** under **Section 80D**. Term Life Insurance premiums qualify for tax exemptions up to **₹1.5 Lakhs** under **Section 80C**, and the final death claim payouts are completely tax-exempt under **Section 10(10D)**.

Our premium health programs connect with HDFC ERGO and ICICI Lombard's cashless networks, spanning over **10,000+ elite hospital panels globally**. Upon admission, presenting your insurance card and pre-authorization form settles medical liabilities directly with the hospital, eliminating out-of-pocket costs.

Luxury asset valuation utilizes an Agreed Value or Market Value schedule. An independent certified appraiser reviews raw asset parameters, authentication certificates, and invoices. The underwriting board then registers a fixed Agreed Sum Insured, bypassing standard depreciation calculations during settlements.

Standard retail health policies require a 3 to 4-year waiting period for pre-existing conditions. However, our prime elite co-underwritten portfolios offer expedited terms, compressing waiting timelines down to **1 to 2 years** or providing immediate cover under special corporate/premium tariff modifications.

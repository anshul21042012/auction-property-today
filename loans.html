Frequently Answered Queries

Essential regulatory and financial standards to qualify for luxury pre-approved funding lines.

To secure prime rates (e.g., SBI Home Loans at 8.40% p.a.), applicants generally require a CIBIL credit score of 750 or higher, an active income profile showing a debt-to-income ratio (FOIR) under 50%, and fully clear title records in the case of collateral-backed secured funding products like Home Loans or LAP.

Fixed rate loans lock in a consistent monthly payments schedule regardless of market dynamics, while Floating rates fluctuate according to bank benchmarks (MCLR or External Benchmark Lending Rates - EBLR/RLLR). Floating options usually offer lower initial thresholds and allow prepayment without penalty chargers.

Our checker employs standard banking underwriting metrics. It utilizes a Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio (FOIR) that scales from 50% to 60% based on your income brackets. This ratio determines how much monthly income is available for debt service, subtracts your active EMIs, and then back-calculates standard borrowing volume metrics.

Commercial files require: 3 years of fully audited balance sheets and profit & loss (P&L) schedules, GST returns for the previous 12 months, bank account statements representing main operational portals, MSME/GST registration licenses, and basic KYC/property papers of primary enterprise promoters.

Transparent Property Auctions

How We Verify Properties

AuctionPropertyToday was conceived to solve the critical transparency and transactional issues that define high-value real estate auctions in India.

Traditionally, property auctions remained locked behind complex legal procedures, inaccessible to buyers due to incomplete title audits and fragmented broker networks.

We bridge this chasm. By partnering directly with India's leading banking boards, we select the best villas, penthouses, and heritage properties, subjecting every property to rigorous audits before listing them on our platform.

Rigorous Standards

The Vetting Audit Cycle

Every property passes three structural checkpoints before listing authorization.

⚖️

1. Judicial Title Clearing

Retired High Court judiciary panels audit past mortgage files and bank recovery papers to guarantee 100% debt-discharge before catalog placement.

📐

2. Physical Structural Audit

Authorized surveyors run high-precision surveys to map built structures and verify structural certificates.

🏦

3. Direct Escrow Securing

We establish verified bank EMD details directly, ensuring all transaction volume paths remain direct, transparent, and secure.
